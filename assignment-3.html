My recipe for best cinnamon rolls

Ingrediants

for the dough :

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2/1 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread floor

for the filling :

  • 2/3 dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the east sugar eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into the dough.
  4. knead the dough for the 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and the cover with plastic cover leaver it somewhere to rise for 24 hours.
  6. Melt the butter for filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll a dough into swiss roll.
  8. Cut the roll into the 3cms sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  9. preheat the oven to 350f or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25 min until the light blown.
